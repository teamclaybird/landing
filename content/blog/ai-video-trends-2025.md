# AI Video Trends for 2025

The landscape of video content creation is evolving rapidly. Here are the top trends we're seeing for 2025.

## 1. AI-Generated Content Goes Mainstream

What was experimental in 2024 is now essential. Brands are using AI to produce hundreds of video variations for A/B testing.

## 2. Vertical Video Dominates

With TikTok, Reels, and Shorts driving engagement, vertical video is no longer optional. It's the primary format for reaching audiences under 35.

## 3. Personalization at Scale

AI enables brands to create personalized video content for different audience segments without exponentially increasing production costs.

## 4. Real-Time Video Editing

Cloud-based AI tools can now edit videos in real-time, allowing for rapid iteration and testing.

## The Bottom Line

Brands that embrace AI-powered video creation will gain a significant competitive advantage in 2025. The question isn't whether to adopt these tools, but how quickly you can integrate them into your workflow.
